Additional Interaction Types

Select all that should be included. Current types: dialog, trade, combat, area.

P

Puzzle

Author defines a riddle, code, or logic challenge. Player must solve it to progress. Can have hints, attempt limits, and failure outcomes.

E

Event / Cutscene

A scripted narrative moment — no player choices, just story beats that play out automatically with optional effects (stat changes, item grants, character reveals).

S

Skill Check

Standalone stat challenge not tied to dialog — pick a lock, climb a wall, sneak past a guard. Branching outcomes based on pass/fail. Dice roll visible to player.

R

Rest / Camp

Player recovers health and resources. Author can attach events that trigger during rest (ambush, dream sequence, NPC conversation).

C

Crafting

Combine items from the player's inventory to create new ones. Author defines recipes. Requires having the right components.

L

Loot

Search a container, body, or hidden cache. Author defines item pools with drop chances. Can require a skill check to find or unlock.

$

Shop

A persistent merchant with a fixed inventory and prices. Distinct from trade — no bartering, just buy/sell with gold. Author defines stock and restock rules.

F

Faction / Reputation

Choices affect standing with named factions. High/low reputation unlocks or blocks future interactions. Author defines factions per tale.
